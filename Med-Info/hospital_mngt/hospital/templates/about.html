About Med-Info

Your Trusted Hospital Management System

Med-Info is a complete and user-friendly Hospital Management System designed specifically for hospitals, clinics, and healthcare centers in Nepal and beyond.

We help you manage everything in one place — patient registration, doctor schedules, appointments, billing, pharmacy, laboratory, staff management, and detailed reports — so your team can focus on providing excellent patient care instead of paperwork.

Why Hospitals Love Med-Info

  • Simple interface — even non-technical staff can use it easily
  • Secure data with user roles and login control
  • Real-time reports and dashboards
  • Affordable pricing for all hospital sizes

Our Mission

"To make hospital management simple, reliable, and affordable — so healthcare providers can spend more time caring for patients and less time managing records."
